The Promise of Safe Passage

Every piece of Sanwariya Gold is a treasure, a fragment of history crafted for the future. We treat its journey with the reverence it deserves. Our shipping policies are meticulously designed to ensure peace of mind, from our vault to your vanity.

local_shipping

Free Standard Delivery

We are pleased to offer complimentary insured shipping on all orders within India. Your bespoke creation will be dispatched within 7-10 business days after the final quality inspection. Standard delivery typically takes 3-5 business days depending on your location.

rocket_launch

Express Delivery

For those occasions that simply cannot wait, we offer an expedited shipping service. Orders placed before 12:00 PM IST will be prioritized for same-day dispatch where inventory allows. Express delivery ensures your package arrives within 24-48 hours to major metropolitan areas.

Additional charges apply based on weight and destination.

public

International Shipping

Sanwariya Gold is admired globally. We ship to over 30 countries securely via our logistics partners FedEx and DHL. International shipments are fully insured and tracked. Please note that customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient and vary by country.

inventory_2

Packaging Excellence

Security is as paramount as beauty. Your heirloom arrives in our signature hand-stitched silk-lined box, designed to cradle the jewelry softly. This box is then encased within a wax-sealed, tamper-evident outer package.

  • verified Tamper-proof holographic seal
  • verified Discreet outer labeling for security
  • verified Certificate of Authenticity included
security

High-Value Orders (Above ₹5 Lakh)

For high-value acquisitions, we employ a specialized armored logistics service. Delivery will require dual-authentication upon receipt: a government-issued ID and a unique OTP sent to your registered mobile number on the day of delivery.

Still have questions about your delivery?

Our concierge team is available Mon-Sat, 10am - 7pm IST to assist with tracking or special requests.
